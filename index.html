Velcome to Community Science Museum

Step inside a world built for explorers of all ages, from stargazers and inventors to future biologists, engineers, and dreamers. Here, science isn't something you read about - it is something you touch, try, test, and experience.

With hands-on exhibits, living stories of discovery, and a universe of ideas waiting to be uncovered, the museum invites you to dive in, ask questions, and see the world with new eyes.
