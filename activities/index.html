Activites

Step inside a world built for explorers of all ages, from stargazers and inventors to future biologists, engineers, and dreamers. Here, science isn't something you read about-it's something you touch, try, test, and experience.

With hands-on exhibits, living stories of discovery, and a universe of ideas waiting to be uncovered, the museum invites you to dive in, ask questions, and see the world with new eyes.

Energetica Exhibition on Loan


On loan from the NEMO Science Museum in Amsterdam, the Energetica exhibition is coming to the Community Science Museum.

It's a series of installations that allow visitors to experience the power of the elements as we harness them. From solar energy powering lighting, to 'Wind Island' that shows how turbines are able to use and control wind to create power.

At Community Science Museum week 18 through week 23.
